# Catalogs prepared for the local quotation app

Date: 2026-06-05
Region focus: Russia

Files:
- wiren_board_rf_2026-06-05.csv
- aqara_rf_2026-06-05.csv
- knx_jung_rf_2026-06-05.csv

Notes:
- Prices are retail snapshot prices gathered on 2026-06-05.
- Aqara prices were taken from official product pages on aqara.ru.
- Wiren Board prices were taken from the official pricelist and product pages on wirenboard.com.
- KNX prices were taken from Russian retail pages for Jung KNX equipment on jungstore.ru.
- These files are practical starter catalogs for rough commercial proposals, not full manufacturer master catalogs.
- KNX projects often also need ETS license, bus cable, DIN hardware, power protections, enclosures, and labor lines; those are not included here unless a device itself is listed.
